Return Policy
Our return policy allows returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the returned item. To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order ID.

Shipping Information
We offer free shipping on orders over $50. Standard shipping takes 3-7 business days. Express shipping is available for an additional fee and takes 1-2 business days. We ship to most countries worldwide. Tracking information will be provided once your order ships.

Account Issues
If you're having trouble accessing your account, try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link. If the problem persists, contact our support team with your email address and we'll help you regain access. Make sure to check your spam folder for password reset emails.

Payment Methods
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Payment is processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway. If you have issues with payment processing, please ensure your billing information matches your card details.

Product Warranty
All our products come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty. The warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship. It does not cover damage due to misuse, accidents, or normal wear and tear. To claim warranty service, contact us with your order ID and description of the issue.

Order Modifications
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After this time, orders are processed for shipping and cannot be changed. If you need to modify an order that's already shipped, you can return it once received and place a new order.
